Deviled Ingredients: These are the simple kitchen staples you’ll need to make this basic deviled egg recipe:

  • Eggs: This recipe yields 12 deviled egg halves, so you’ll need six boiled eggs.
  • Mayonnaise: Mayonnaise makes the filling extra creamy.
  • Sugar: A teaspoon of white sugar lends a hint of sweetness.
  • Vinegar: A teaspoon of white vinegar, meanwhile, cuts the sweetness with acidity.
  • Mustard: Use store-bought prepared mustard or homemade yellow mustard.
  • Vegetables: You’ll need an onion and celery.
  • Seasonings: These deviled eggs are seasoned with salt and paprika.

Steps followed:

  1. How Long to Boil Eggs for Deviled Eggs Perfect deviled eggs start with perfectly hard-cooked eggs. We recommend following our top-rated Hard-Boiled Eggs recipe, which takes about 50 minutes from beginning to end.
  2. Cut the boiled eggs in half and remove the yolks. Reserve the whites.
  3. Mash the yolks with a fork and add the remaining ingredients.
  4. Stuff or pipe the filling into the egg white halves.
